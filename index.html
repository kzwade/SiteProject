Breads

White Bread

Ingriedients

  • ll c. flour
  • 2 T. yeast
  • 6 T. sugar
  • 6 T. oil
  • 4 T. salt
  • 1 c. milk
  • 3 c. water
  • Equipment: kettlee, large mixing bowl, large mixing spoon (wooden), 4 cup glass measuring cup, plastic wrap

Steps

  1. Put water in a kettle and turn on to heat up.
  2. Put 4 cups of flour and all other dry ingriedients into a very large bowl and stir to combine.
  3. Pour oil and milk into large glass measuring cup.
  4. Start adding the 3 cups of hot water.
  5. Mix the liquids into the flour with a large spoon.
  6. Pour remaing flour into the bowl, stirring as you go along.
  7. Stop adding flour or add more flour to get a consistency where it is no longer sticking to your fingers.
  8. Knead about 10 minutes.
  9. Stop when it's satiny and it spring back right away when you press on it.
  10. Pour oil around the edge of the bowl.
  11. Cover the bowl with a plastic wrap and then a towel.
  12. If you are in a hurry, put in a warm place.
  13. After it has doubled in size, punch it down and form it how you would like to cook it.
  14. Let it rise another 40 minutes before baking at 400 degrees in the oven for 20 minutes/.

Soups

Red Lentil Soup

Ingriedients

  • 2 carrots
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 1/2 c. red lentils
  • 1 T. Vegeta
  • salt and pepper as desired
  • Equipment: soup pot, stirring spoon, sharp knife, grater

Steps

  1. Grate two carrots.
  2. Finely dice a medium onion.
  3. Saute the onion in a splotch of oil in the pot.
  4. Add 7 cups of water, the carrots, and lentils.
  5. Season with Vegeta.
  6. Bring it to a boil and then turn to the lowest simmer.
  7. It will take about an hour for the lentils to cook.

Notes: You can let it cook for as long as you'd like, but keep it on very low heat and stir whenever you walk past it.
